More About Us


My story begins my senior year of high school when I discovered my passion for creating unique and inviting scents. What started as a simple hobby—experimenting with essential oils and natural ingredients—quickly turned into an obsession. I found joy in blending fragrances that evoked memories, emotions, and comfort.

With encouragement from friends and family, I decided to turn my passion into something bigger. Scents by Scott was born out of a desire to share high-quality, homemade scents with others. Every fragrance is handcrafted with care, using only the finest ingredients to ensure a luxurious and long-lasting experience.

I aim for my small kitchen project brand to bring warmth, relaxation and aura for my clients. Thank you for being part of this journey—I can't wait for you to experience the magic of Scents by Scott!
